An unconventional recital of music by Bach, Corelli, Purcell and others, led by historical performance specialist Maggie Faultless (violin), alongside Rachel Stroud (violin), Andrew Skidmore (cello) and Nick Mulroy (tenor). This event, set in an intimate, informal space, invited a different kind of listening experience entirely - sitting close to our performers, on sofas, cushions, the floor, and encouraged to stand and let the music flow through your limbs for the closing wild strains of Antonio Vivaldi's _Folia_.
