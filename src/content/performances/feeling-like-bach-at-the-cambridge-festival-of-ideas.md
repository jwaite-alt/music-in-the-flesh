Feeling (Like) Bach?

We all know how music can move us, but Bach’s music in its own time had the power to bring about profound transformations in a listener’s physical and spiritual state. Nowadays, Bach’s music tends to be understood more as a disembodied object of aesthetic contemplation. 

In this workshop, we played through selected excerpts from Bach's work with a group of specialist musicians, to inspire our listeners to experience for themselves some of the transformative effects it can produce.
